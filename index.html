About the ISSU

The Indian Subcontinental Student Union (ISSU) is a student-led organization dedicated to celebrating the culture and traditions of the Indian subcontinent. We host events like Diwali and Holi and create merchandise to spread awareness. We meet during C1 in Eller 215.
